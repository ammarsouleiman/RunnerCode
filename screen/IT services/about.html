About us

Since its inception in 2022, runner_code has become a strong force in the IT services, software, web development and online education sectors. runner_code believes that customer experience is the key to our success. For this reason, all of our employees are fully trained in customer service. runner_code was founded on the premise that IT services are constantly evolving and getting support is not easy anymore. Why can't information technology be easily accessible to everyone? Why are there so many issues with technology, and what can we do to make things easier for the employer? What can we take from the technology we have today and make everything easier to use? These are all questions that were asked when founding runner_code.

Better Value

runner_code only provides services that our clients need. We do not oversell services. By offering prices that are less expensive and services which are more vast than our competition, we provide an incredible value to our customers.
